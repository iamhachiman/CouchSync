# CouchSync 🛋️📲💻

CouchSync is a seamless tethering and cross-device connectivity application designed specifically for those who want to keep their productivity (or laziness!) at peak levels. It bridges the gap between your Android device and Windows PC, ensuring you never have to pick up your phone when you are at your desk.

## 🚀 Vision
The goal of CouchSync is to create a unified experience where your phone's capabilities are natively accessible from your Windows environment. Whether it's managing notifications, using your phone as a high-quality webcam, or sharing media on the fly, CouchSync handles it all.

## ✨ Project Repositories
- **[CouchSync-Android](https://github.com/iamhachiman/CouchSync-Android)**: The Android app for sending notifications and more.
- **[CouchSync-Windows](https://github.com/iamhachiman/CouchSync-Windows)**: The Windows app for receiving and displaying data.
